What Are Urea Filters?

Urea filters are essential components in Diesel Exhaust Fluid (DEF) systems, removing impurities from urea solutions to protect SCR catalysts, dosing pumps, and injector nozzles. Clean DEF ensures proper NOx reduction, improves engine performance, and prolongs system life in vehicles and industrial equipment.
