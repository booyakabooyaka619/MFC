What Are Fuel Filters?

Fuel filters are essential components designed to remove dirt, rust, water, and other contaminants from fuel before it reaches the engine. By ensuring clean fuel delivery, they protect injectors and pumps, improve combustion efficiency, reduce wear, and enhance overall engine reliability and longevity.
