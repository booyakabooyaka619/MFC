Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our filtration solutions and services.

What types of filters do you manufacture?
We manufacture a comprehensive range of filters including Air Filters, Fuel Filters, Fuel Water Separators, Oil Filters, Steering Filters, Urea Filters, and Hydraulic Filters for automotive, marine, industrial, and heavy equipment applications.
Do you provide custom filtration solutions?
Yes, we specialize in custom filtration solutions. Our engineering team can design and manufacture filters to meet your specific requirements, including unique dimensions, flow rates, and filtration specifications.
What is your typical lead time for orders?
Lead times vary depending on the product type and quantity. Standard filters typically ship within 5-7 business days, while custom solutions may take 2-4 weeks. We'll provide accurate timing when you place your order.
Do you offer technical support and consultation?
Absolutely! Our technical team provides comprehensive support including filter selection guidance, application analysis, and ongoing technical consultation to ensure optimal performance of your filtration systems.
